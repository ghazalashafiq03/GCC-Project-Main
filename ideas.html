Virtual Assistant

A virtual assistant is generally self-employed and provides professional administrative, technical, or creative assistance to clients remotely from a home office.



Lawn Care Specialist

Lawn care specialists work for lawn care companies or for themselves. They work alone or in teams, providing all sorts of different services to private homes, governments, hospitals and corporate buildings. A lawn care specialist will mow grass, seed lawns, apply fertilizer and pest control, and trim hedges and trees.

Freelance Writer

A freelance writer is a writer who works on a self-employed basis. They can work for just one magazine or, more often, they write for several different publications at a time. The more diverse a writer can be, the more likely they are to be published and paid for their work.
